Junior Research Fellow (JRF)

Open

The Wearable Robotics & Control (WeRoCon) Laboratory, Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur, invites applications for the position of Junior Research Fellow (JRF).

The selected candidate will contribute to the development of intelligent and robust control strategies for lower-limb prostheses and powered exoskeletons. The project focuses on the design, implementation, and experimental validation of advanced control algorithms for wearable robotic systems, with the objective of improving human mobility and rehabilitation.

PhD Positions

Open

The Wearable Robotics & Control (WeRoCon) Laboratory at the IIT Jodhpur invites applications for a fully funded PhD position in developing intelligent and robust control strategies for lower-limb prostheses and exoskeletons. The successful candidate will work on the design and development of these wearable robots, with a focus on implementing and experimentally validating advanced control strategies for powered prosthetic legs and exoskeletons.

If you are interested in joining, please send an email to Dr. Kumar along with a cover letter and CV.
